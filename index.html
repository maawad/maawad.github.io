Muhammad Awad is a Ph.D. student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the University of California, Davis. He received his B.Sc. degree in Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering from the Faculty of Engineering, Alexandria University, Egypt in 2013. After receiving his bachelor's degree, he worked for two years as a part-time teaching assistant at the Arab Academy for Science, Technology, and Maritime Transport, in addition to doing research on point sampling, and mesh generation and optimization. Currently, he is working on his Ph.D. under the supervision of Professor John D. Owens. His research interests include mesh generation and optimization techniques, parallel programming, and computer graphics.
